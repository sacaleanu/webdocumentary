I'm doing it my way

"I like doing exactly what I wanna do, and not what everybody else expects me to do."

ERUM is an avantgarde metal band from Aarhus. Fusing saxophones with drums and detuned guitarriffs ERUM creates a unique and powerfull sound, that challenges the norms of jazz and metal. The band is a quintet consisting of two saxophones, 1 drummer, 1 bassist and 2 guitarist.

ERUM's debut album features six very strong compositions. Several more pieces were recorded, but these six was chosen out as the best. The compositions consists of a mixture of dark riffs, sinister saxophones and hard hitting drumbeats. The album is an unloading of energy, and there is not a lot of room for peace. It is all about expressing and unloading hard hitting energy!
